Invite Discord Source Code Support Server

Your personal In-House Games manager

A Discord Bot Designed to organise In-House custom games. Inspired by LoL Champions Queue.
Currently, designed for League of Legends. But will work for any other 5v5 game.
Features;
  • Supports Brand-new Discord slash commands
  • Global Ranking system
  • Role assignment per Queue
  • Ready Up! Time to ready up appears in Timezone of the player
  • Game-specific Voice and Lobby channels created
  • Game-specific Voice and Lobby automatically deleted on game conclusion
  • Spectators - With a click of a button, spectators are allowed to join one team voice channel

Take your server to the next level

Provide a Competitive environment for your Discord server. Sit back and let the Bot create, manage and track all In-House custom games. Members are able to track their progress through the built-in leaderboard.
